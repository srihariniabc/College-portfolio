ADMISSION 2023-2024

    PSGR Krishnammal College for Women (PSGRKCW) pursues a holistic admission policy for all the program(s) offered by the institution. A holistic admission policy seeks to understand the whole applicant beyond marks and solicit whether an applicant will be a good fit in a program and whether they will thrive. This is in keeping with the best practices of International Universities and allows us to select the best applicant for our program(s).

    ADMISSION POLICY AND PROCESS

  1. All the candidates seeking admission to the Undergraduate (UG) / Postgraduate (PG) are required to register online or can visit to the College and receive the printed application.

  2. Admission is made purely on the basis of merit, subject to the rules of reservation of the Government of Tamil Nadu and Bharathiar University.

  3. As per the present policy, a candidate seeking admission in Undergraduate (UG) programmes must have passed 12th examination of 10+2 system from a recognised board and seeking admission in Postgraduate (PG) programmes must have passed UG programme from a recognised Institution/University.

  4. The applicants will be eligible to take admission in all the program(s) provided they satisfy the cut-off and the eligibility for the selected program.

  5. If applicants have examination results pending or have reappeared in papers, they will be able to update these marks by logging into their Dashboards. The college may be contacted for further details of admissions.

  6. Applicants will be responsible for the quality and authenticity of all the information they enter, including upload of copies of certificates.

  7. Admissions will be considered to be complete only when the full fee for the academic year is paid (against an official receipt) and the name of the applicant appears on the relevant rolls.
